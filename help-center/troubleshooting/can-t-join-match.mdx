---
title: Can't Join a Match
description: Fix issues joining games or tournaments.
---

## Symptoms

- "Unable to join match" error
- Lobby remains stuck on loading

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your connection">
    ````bash
    ping wamba.gg
    # or test streaming services
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the page">
    Retry joining the match after refreshing your browser or the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Log out and clear your browser cache, then log back in.
  </Step>
</Steps>
