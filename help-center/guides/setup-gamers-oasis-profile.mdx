---
title: Setting Up Your Gamers Oasis Profile
description: Create and customize your esports profile.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to Gamers Oasis">
    Go to `https://oasis.wamba.gg` and log in with your Wamba account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your profile">
    Click your avatar > "Edit Profile". Add a gamer tag, avatar, and bio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link game accounts">
    Connect your Steam, Xbox, or PlayStation accounts for verified stats.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save settings">
    Click "Save". Your profile is now ready to compete!
  </Step>
</Steps>
