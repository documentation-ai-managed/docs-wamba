## Create an Account

1. Go to `https://wamba.gg` and click "Sign Up"
2. Enter your email and set a password
3. Confirm your email address

## Join Gamers Oasis Platform

- Sign in with your new account
- Create or join esports tournaments
- Explore available games and competitions

Need help? Contact [support@wamba.gg](mailto:support@wamba.gg)
